About the Event

SRET Culturals is an annual celebration of creativity, talent, and tradition, hosted by SRET College. The event brings together students from all departments to showcase their skills in dance, music, drama, art, and more. It's a vibrant mix of competitions, performances, and fun-filled activities that highlight youthful spirit and unity. Join us for an unforgettable day of culture, energy, and celebration at its best!
