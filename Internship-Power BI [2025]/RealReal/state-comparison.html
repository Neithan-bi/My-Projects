Drill-down Column Chart

This interactive chart allows users to explore COVID-19 data hierarchically. You can click on a state to drill down and view detailed metrics by district (if available). In cases where district-level data isn't present, the chart shows other metric types such as active, recovered, and deceased cases. This enhances user insights by enabling a deeper, region-specific analysis.

Word Cloud

States are visualized as words, with their size reflecting the selected metric—like active cases or deaths. Larger names indicate higher values, making it easy to spot trends at a glance.
